================================================================
STEP 4 — Leave Management   [ 5 min ]
================================================================

As Employee   (/employee/apply-leaves):
  - Login as Employee
  - Apply a leave — select type, dates, and reason
  - Show leave quota: Annual 15 days, Casual 12 days, Sick 12

As Manager   (/manager/leaves-request):
  - Login as Manager
  - Show team's pending leave requests
  - Approve one leave, reject one with a reason

As Admin   (/admin/leaves-status):
  - Show all company leaves in one view

Approval Flow:
  Employee applies  →  Manager approves/rejects  →  Admin sees all

Key Points to Say:
  "The entire leave workflow is digital — employee applies,
   manager reviews and approves or rejects, admin has full
   visibility. No emails, no phone calls."

  "Leave balance is automatically deducted when approved —
   the system tracks everything."

  "When a leave is rejected, the reason is sent to the
   employee — complete transparency."

  "Unpaid leaves are automatically considered in the
   monthly payslip calculation."

Highlight:
  ✅ 3-level digital approval workflow
  ✅ Auto leave balance deduction on approval
  ✅ Reject with reason — professional process
  ✅ Unpaid leave auto-deducted in payroll

COMMON CLIENT QUESTIONS & ANSWERS
================================================================

Q: How secure is the data?
A: All data is stored in MongoDB cloud database. Files are on
   Cloudinary (encrypted). JWT authentication is used. The
   application runs over HTTPS. Unauthorized access is not
   possible.

Q: How many employees can it handle?
A: MongoDB is a scalable cloud database. It can handle
   thousands of employees without any performance issues and
   scales automatically as the organization grows.

Q: Will it work on mobile?
A: Yes, the application has a responsive design and works
   smoothly on mobile and tablet browsers.

Q: Can we add our company branding?
A: Yes — logo, colors, and company name can all be customized.
   A fully white-label version is possible.

Q: Will employees need training?
A: The system is very simple and intuitive. One walkthrough
   session is enough. We also provide onboarding support.

Q: Are the tax calculations accurate?
A: Tax slabs are defined by the admin according to the
   company's own rules — fully customizable, no hardcoded
   formulas.
